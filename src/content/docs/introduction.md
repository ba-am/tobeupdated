Welcome to **AI Nexus**. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive framework for architecting and engineering the next generation of intelligent systems.

## Why AI Nexus?

In a world where AI is rapidly becoming the core of software functionality, traditional engineering patterns often fall short. We focus on:

- **Probabilistic Reasoning**: Moving beyond deterministic logic.
- **System Resilience**: Ensuring AI systems are safe and predictable.
- **Architectural Scalability**: Designing for data and model growth.
